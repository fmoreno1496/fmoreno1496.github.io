How We’ll Know It’s Working

The story, measured.

The measures only matter if they protect the story. Checked at Day 30, 45 and 90 across four dimensions: academic progress, relationship, clarity & direction, belief & confidence.

Day 30

Jordan is engaged, has completed .50 credits, and no drift signals are ignored.

Day 45

Jordan has a first win, names a support person, and can describe his future direction.

Day 90

Jordan has 1.5+ credits, attended a workshop, and believes graduation is in motion.

KNOW

His plan, his credits, his first win, and exactly what it takes to keep going.

FEEL

Seen, connected, confident, and supported by people who believe in his success.

ACCOMPLISHED

At least one course complete, a workshop attended, real conversations with his GCA and coaches.

BELIEVE

That he belongs here, that he can do this, and that graduating is a plan already in motion.
